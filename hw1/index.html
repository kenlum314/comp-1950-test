Motivation for Taking COMP 1950

Although I gained practical knowledge and experience from working on the above projects, I found myself lacking in some areas, particularly with respect to the front-end (I mostly deal with the middle tier portions of a system). Realizing I had no formal training in front-end development, I decided to take COMP 1950 because I wished to gain more familiarity in this area, namely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Because I already have some on-the-job training in addition to courses I took for my computer science degree, I decided not to start with COMP 1850, but with this course.

I may end up using the credit from this course towards the associate certificate program in Web and Mobile Application Development.
